## 🎯 **Course Structure Summary**

### **8 Units × 4 Lessons Each = 32 Total Lessons**

#### **UNIT 1: First Day** (4 lessons)
- School supplies and basic shapes

#### **UNIT 2: Art Class** (4 lessons)  
- Art supplies and colors
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Phonics Bonus (after Unit 2)**

#### **UNIT 3: Birthday Party** (4 lessons)
- Numbers 1-12 and toys

#### **UNIT 4: Home** (4 lessons)
- Family members and food
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Phonics Bonus (after Unit 4)**

#### **UNIT 5: The Park** (4 lessons)
- Nature and playtime activities

#### **UNIT 6: The Zoo** (4 lessons)
- Zoo animals and abilities
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Phonics Bonus (after Unit 6)**

#### **UNIT 7: Science Day** (4 lessons)
- Body parts and healthy habits

#### **UNIT 8: The Toy Store** (4 lessons)
- Adjectives and transportation
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Phonics Bonus (after Unit 8)**

### **Assessment Structure:**
- **4 Assessment Points:** After Units 2, 4, 6, and 8
- **Each Assessment Includes:**
  - Check Up (skills review)
  - Phonics Bonus (phonics practice)

### **Recurring Elements:**
- **Stories** - Interactive dialogues for each lesson
- **Check Up** - Assessment pages every 2 units
- **Phonics Bonus** - Additional phonics practice
- **Values** - Character building components (Be friendly, Be nice, Be fair, Be kind, Be helpful, Be safe, Be polite)

### **Skills Integration:**
- **Speaking** - Question-answer patterns and dialogues
- **Listening** - Story comprehension and vocabulary recognition
- **Reading** - Word recognition and simple sentences
- **Writing** - Basic word formation and copying
- **Grammar** - This/that, these/those, singular/plural, can/can't
- **Vocabulary** - 150+ new words building on Level 0 foundation

## 🎯 **Level-Specific Information**

### **Target Proficiency Level:**
- **Level 1 Focus:** Building on Start Level foundation with more complex grammar

### **Cambridge Integration:**
- **Target Exam:** Cambridge A1 Starter preparation
- **Skills Focus:** A1 level competency development
- **Assessment Readiness:** Building toward A1 Starter exam readiness

### **Prerequisites:**
- **Required Background:** Completion of Start Level (Level 0)
- **Recommended Skills:** Basic alphabet knowledge, simple vocabulary

### **Learning Outcomes:**
- **Reading Skills:** Simple sentences and basic text comprehension
- **Writing Skills:** Basic sentence formation and word writing
- **Speaking Skills:** Question-answer exchanges and simple conversations
- **Listening Skills:** Story comprehension and instruction following
- **Grammar Mastery:** This/that/these/those, singular/plural forms, can/can't
- **Vocabulary Acquisition:** 150+ words across 8 thematic units
