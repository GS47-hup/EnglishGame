- **4 Assessment Points:** After Units 2, 4, 6, and 8

  - Check Up (comprehensive skills review)
  - Skills Bonus (advanced practice and application)

- **Reading** - Complex academic and literary texts
- **Check Up** - Comprehensive assessment every 2 units
- **Skills Bonus** - Advanced academic skills development
- **Values** - Character building (Be careful, Be helpful, Be on time, Be thoughtful, Be polite, Be patient)

- **Speaking** - Academic discussions and formal presentations
- **Listening** - Academic lectures and complex audio materials
- **Reading** - Academic texts, literature, and technical materials
- **Writing** - Academic essays, reports, and creative compositions
