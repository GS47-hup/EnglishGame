## 🎯 **Course Structure Summary**

### **8 Units × 4 Lessons Each = 32 Total Lessons**

#### **UNIT 1: Fun Outdoors** (4 lessons)
- Outdoor activities and sports

#### **UNIT 2: Land and Sea** (4 lessons)  
- Animals, sea creatures, and measurements
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Skills Bonus (after Unit 2)**

#### **UNIT 3: What We Look Like** (4 lessons)
- Physical appearance and accessories

#### **UNIT 4: Last Week** (4 lessons)
- Past tense activities and sports
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Skills Bonus (after Unit 4)**

#### **UNIT 5: A Day Out** (4 lessons)
- Food, activities, and past experiences

#### **UNIT 6: Being Creative** (4 lessons)
- Arts, crafts, and creative activities
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Skills Bonus (after Unit 6)**

#### **UNIT 7: Things to Be** (4 lessons)
- Future careers and aspirations

#### **UNIT 8: On Vacation** (4 lessons)
- Travel plans and vacation activities
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Skills Bonus (after Unit 8)**

### **Assessment Structure:**
- **4 Assessment Points:** After Units 2, 4, 6, and 8
- **Each Assessment Includes:**
  - Check Up (skills review)
  - Skills Bonus (additional practice)

### **Recurring Elements:**
- **Reading** - Extended stories and comprehension
- **Check Up** - Assessment pages every 2 units
- **Skills Bonus** - Advanced skills practice
- **Values** - Character building components (Be careful, Be respectful, Be kind, Be prepared, Be helpful, Be patient, Be thoughtful)

### **Skills Integration:**
- **Speaking** - Future plans, past experiences, and detailed descriptions
- **Listening** - Extended dialogue and story comprehension
- **Reading** - Multi-paragraph texts and reading comprehension
- **Writing** - Complex sentences and narrative writing
- **Grammar** - Future with "going to", past tense mastery, comparatives and superlatives
- **Vocabulary** - 250+ advanced words across specialized topics
