## 🎯 **Course Structure Summary**

### **8 Units × 4 Lessons Each = 32 Total Lessons**

#### **UNIT 1: How We Feel** (4 lessons)
- Emotions and feelings vocabulary

#### **UNIT 2: In Town** (4 lessons)  
- Jobs and places vocabulary
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Checkup + Young Kids Exam (after Unit 2)**

#### **UNIT 3: Things to Eat** (4 lessons)
- Food and fruit vocabulary

#### **UNIT 4: Things to Wear** (4 lessons)
- Clothing items vocabulary
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Checkup + Young Kids Exam (after Unit 4)**

#### **UNIT 5: Things to Do** (4 lessons)
- Actions and activities vocabulary

#### **UNIT 6: Home** (4 lessons)
- Household items and rooms vocabulary
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Checkup + Young Kids Exam (after Unit 6)**

#### **UNIT 7: My Day** (4 lessons)
- Time and daily routines vocabulary

#### **UNIT 8: My Week** (4 lessons)
- School subjects and classes vocabulary
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Checkup + Young Kids Exam (after Unit 8)**

### **Assessment Structure:**
- **4 Assessment Points:** After Units 2, 4, 6, and 8
- **Each Assessment Includes:**
  - Checkup (skills review)
  - Young Kids Exam (formal assessment)

### **Recurring Elements:**
- **Stories** - Engaging narratives for each lesson
- **Check Up** - Assessment pages every 2 units
- **Young Kids Exam** - Formal assessment every 2 units
- **Phonics Bonus** - Additional phonics practice
- **Values** - Character building components

### **Skills Integration:**
- **Speaking** - Conversation practice
- **Listening** - Story comprehension
- **Reading** - Text and dialogue reading
- **Writing** - Guided writing activities
- **Grammar** - Progressive grammar introduction
- **Vocabulary** - Systematic vocabulary building
