## 🎯 **Course Structure Summary**

### **8 Units × 4 Lessons Each = 32 Total Lessons**

#### **UNIT 1: School Supplies** (4 lessons)
- Basic school materials vocabulary

#### **UNIT 2: Toys** (4 lessons)  
- Common toys and play items
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Young Learners Exam (after Unit 2)**

#### **UNIT 3: Colors** (4 lessons)
- Basic color vocabulary

#### **UNIT 4: Numbers** (4 lessons)
- Numbers 1-10 and counting
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Young Learners Exam (after Unit 4)**

#### **UNIT 5: Animals** (4 lessons)
- Pets and farm animals

#### **UNIT 6: Food** (4 lessons)
- Basic food and drinks
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Young Learners Exam (after Unit 6)**

#### **UNIT 7: Abilities** (4 lessons)
- Physical actions and abilities

#### **UNIT 8: My Body** (4 lessons)
- Body parts and movements
- **→ ASSESSMENT: Check Up + Young Learners Exam (after Unit 8)**

### **Assessment Structure:**
- **4 Assessment Points:** After Units 2, 4, 6, and 8
- **Each Assessment Includes:**
  - Check Up (skills review)
  - Young Learners Exam (formal assessment)

### **Recurring Elements:**
- **Stories** - Simple dialogues for each lesson
- **Check Up** - Assessment pages every 2 units
- **Young Learners Exam** - Formal assessment every 2 units
- **Phonics** - Complete A-Z alphabet coverage
- **Values** - Character building components (Be friendly, Be polite, Be nice, Be helpful, Be fair)

### **Skills Integration:**
- **Speaking** - Basic conversation patterns
- **Listening** - Simple dialogue comprehension
- **Reading** - Letter recognition and phonics
- **Writing** - Letter formation and basic words
- **Grammar** - Simple present tense, basic questions
- **Vocabulary** - Foundation vocabulary building (100+ words)

---

**📌 Note:** This syllabus represents the complete Start Level (Level 0) curriculum for absolute beginners, focusing on foundational English skills and vocabulary introduction.
