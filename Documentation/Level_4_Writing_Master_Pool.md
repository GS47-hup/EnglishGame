## ✍️ Part 2: Write a Short Story (12 Prompts)
*Instructions: Choose ONE topic and write 5-7 sentences. Use correct grammar and vocabulary from Level 4.*

1.  **Topic**: My Last Weekend (What did you do? Who did you play with?)
2.  **Topic**: My Favorite Sport (What is it? Why do you like it? Are you good at it?)
3.  **Topic**: My Vacation Plans (Where are you going? What are you going to take?)
4.  **Topic**: My Best Friend (What does he/she look like? What does he/she like doing?)
5.  **Topic**: My Dream Job (What do you want to be? Why? What do you want to do?)
6.  **Topic**: A Visit to the Zoo (What animals did you see? Which was the biggest/fastest?)
7.  **Topic**: My Favorite Hobby (What do you like to do in your free time? Why?)
8.  **Topic**: Being Creative (What can you make? What did you make yesterday?)
9.  **Topic**: A Day at the Beach (What did you eat/drink? What did you see?)
10. **Topic**: My School Trip (Where did you go? How was it?)
11. **Topic**: Staying Safe (What do you wear to be safe? Why is it important?)
12. **Topic**: Animals and Me (What is your favorite animal? What does it look like?)
