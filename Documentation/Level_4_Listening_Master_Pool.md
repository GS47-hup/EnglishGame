## 🖼️ Part 3: Multiple Choice Pictures (Q31-50)
*Instructions: Listen to the conversation and choose the correct picture (A, B, or C).*

**31. What did she eat for lunch?**
*   **Transcript**: "Hey Sarah, did you eat sushi for lunch?" / "No, I didn't. I ate a big bowl of noodles."
*   **A**: Noodles | **B**: Sushi | **C**: Curry

**32. What does he look like?**
*   **Transcript**: "Is that your brother?" / "Yes, he has short, straight hair and he wears glasses."
*   **A**: Curly hair | **B**: Straight hair + glasses | **C**: Long hair

**33. What sport is he good at?**
*   **Transcript**: "Are you good at skiing, Peter?" / "No, I'm not. But I'm very good at skateboarding!"
*   **A**: Ice skating | **B**: Skateboarding | **C**: Skiing

**34. What does she want to be?**
*   **Transcript**: "Do you want to be a scientist, Maria?" / "No, I want to be an artist and paint beautiful pictures."
*   **A**: Artist | **B**: Scientist | **C**: Musician

**35. What is he going to do tomorrow?**
*   **Transcript**: "Are you going to go bowling tomorrow?" / "No, I'm going to go to the park to see a parade."
*   **A**: Bowling | **B**: Taking pictures | **C**: Seeing a parade
