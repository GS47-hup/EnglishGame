## ‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL TEACHER REMINDER: "THIS IS" vs "THESE ARE"**

**Students MUST differentiate:**
- **"THIS IS"** = ONE item ‚Üí "This is a book" / "This is my father"
- **"THESE ARE"** = MULTIPLE items ‚Üí "These are books" / "These are my parents"

**Common mistakes:** ‚ùå "This is books" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "These are books"

**Goal:** Students choose correct form naturally by end of review.

### üéØ **GAME 1: FLASHCARD REVOLUTION**
**Target:** All 85+ vocabulary words + sentence patterns  
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes  

#### **4 Flashcard Modes:**

**Mode A: Visual Lightning**
- **Format:** Word ‚Üí Image/Emoji
- **Method:** 3-second reveals, immediate recognition
- **Vocabulary Sets:** 
  - Family (8 words): father, mother, brother, sister, etc.
  - Colors (8 words): red, blue, yellow, green, etc.
  - Body Parts (10 words): head, hair, eyes, nose, etc.
  - House (9 words): house, door, window, room, etc.
  - Toys (8 words): ball, doll, car, teddy bear, etc.

**Mode B: Audio ‚Üí Word Recognition**
- **Format:** Audio prompt ‚Üí Choose correct written word
- **Integration:** Listen to "This is my father" ‚Üí Select "father"
- **Pattern Practice:** "What color is this?" ‚Üí [RED] ‚Üí "It's red"

**Mode C: Sentence Builder**
- **Format:** Word cards ‚Üí Build complete sentences
- **Examples:** 
  - [This] [is] [my] [blue] [car] = "This is my blue car"
  - [There] [are] [two] [red] [balls] = "There are two red balls"
  - [My] [sister] [has] [brown] [hair] = "My sister has brown hair"

**Mode D: Category Speed Sort**
- **Method:** Drag words into correct categories in 60 seconds
- **Categories:** Family, Colors, Body Parts, House, Toys, School, Actions
- **Challenge:** Mix all 85+ words, sort correctly under time pressure

### üéØ **GAME 2: FAMILY DETECTIVE** 
**Target:** Day 1 - Family vocabulary + "This is/These are" patterns  
**Duration:** 8 minutes

#### **Game Mechanics:**
**Round 1: Who's Missing?** (3 min)
- Show family photos with one member hidden
- Student identifies: "This is my [family member]"
- Practice: "Who's this?" ‚Üí "This is my father/mother/sister"

**Round 2: Family Descriptions** (3 min)
- Family members with different hair/eye colors
- Complete sentences: "My father has brown hair and blue eyes"
- Pattern focus: "My [family member] has [color] [body part]"

**Round 3: Singular vs Plural Challenge** (2 min)
- Critical practice: "This is" vs "These are"
- Show 1 person ‚Üí "This is my brother"
- Show 2+ people ‚Üí "These are my parents/grandparents"
